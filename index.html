ABOUT

Quick honesty about how I work

My day job is customer experience and workforce management at Nations of Sky, a real-estate developer in Cairo. My real hobby is fixing "wouldn't it be nice if…" complaints by writing the thing before someone else does. That's how most of the projects above got started.

I don't have a computer science degree. I learned by shipping, breaking things, and rewriting them cleaner the next weekend. Everything I build runs entirely from a browser — I don't own a Mac or a development laptop. Whatever I hand you was made in GitHub's web editor, deployed on Vercel or GitHub Pages, with Supabase doing the heavy lifting behind it.

SoloTec is the studio name. It's a promise: you talk to the person writing the code, and one person owns the whole thing end-to-end. No handoffs, no translation loss, no team meetings about meetings.
